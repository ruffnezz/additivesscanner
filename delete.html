Delete Account and Data Policy

At Eat Safe, we value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Submit a Request: Email us at artthaiisht@gmail.com with the subject line “Delete My Account.”
  2. Include Details: Provide the email address associated with your account to help us identify and remove your data.
  3. Processing Time: Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of receiving your request.

Data We Collect

  • Email address (if used for login via Google Firebase Authentication)
  • Anonymous user ID from Firebase

Data Retention

  • Upon request, all user-related data is permanently deleted within 7 days.
  • No data is retained after deletion or shared with third parties.

If you have any questions, contact us at artthaiisht@gmail.com.
